## 🎮 First Time Playing?

### Getting Started
1. **Main Menu** - View your stats (current level, stars, money earned)
2. **Click "Start Level 1"** - Begin your cashier career
3. **Read Tutorial** - Click "How to Play" for detailed instructions

### Basic Gameplay (30 seconds to master!)

#### Step 1: Scan Items
- **Option A**: Type barcode (e.g., "4131") and press Enter
- **Option B**: Click product icons to instantly scan

#### Step 2: Build the Cart
- Watch for the customer's order
- Scan all items they want
- Remove mistakes by clicking the X button

#### Step 3: Process Payment
1. Click "Proceed to Payment"
2. Choose payment method:
   - **Card**: Instant, no change needed
   - **Cash**: Enter amount, give correct change

#### Step 4: Next Customer!
- Cart clears automatically
- Next customer appears
- Repeat until all customers served!

## 💡 Pro Tips & Tricks

### Speed Tips
1. **Use product clicks** instead of typing barcodes
2. **Scan while customer arrives** (prep time)
3. **Use card payment** for fastest checkouts
4. **Memorize common products** (Apple = 4131, Banana = 4011)

### Accuracy Tips
1. **Double-check cart** before proceeding to payment
2. **Watch customer mood** - they show what they want
3. **Count items** - Match quantity to order
4. **Use the X button** - Don't be afraid to remove items

### Patience Management
1. **Prioritize current customer** - Queue waits automatically
2. **Watch patience meter** - Red = danger zone
3. **Speed up on impatient customers** (😤)
4. **Rush hour = lower patience** for everyone

### Payment Strategy
1. **Card = Fast** - Use when time is tight
2. **Cash = Practice** - Good for learning
3. **Round numbers** - Enter $10 for $9.99 total
4. **Double-check change** - Calculator in your head!

## 🛠️ Troubleshooting

### Game Not Loading?
1. Check console for errors (F12)
2. Verify you're on the correct URL
3. Clear browser cache and reload
4. Try different browser (Chrome/Firefox/Edge)

### Sound Not Working?
1. Click anywhere on page first (browser policy)
2. Check sound toggle in main menu
3. Verify browser volume isn't muted
4. Some browsers block Web Audio by default

### Progress Not Saving?
1. Enable LocalStorage in browser settings
2. Disable private/incognito mode
3. Check browser storage quota
4. Try clearing old data: `localStorage.clear()`

### Performance Issues?
1. Close other browser tabs
2. Disable browser extensions temporarily
3. Reduce animation settings (future feature)
4. Use desktop instead of mobile for best performance
