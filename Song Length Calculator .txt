BPM to Song Length Calculator
A BPM to song length calculator estimates track duration based on tempo.
Formula:
* Song Length = (Total Beats ÷ BPM)
Example:
* BPM = 120
* Beats = 480
Length = 480 ÷ 120 = 4 minutes
This is helpful for:
* Music producers
* DJs syncing tracks
* Beat creators
