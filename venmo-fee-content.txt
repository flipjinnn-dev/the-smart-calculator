How to Avoid or Reduce Venmo Fees
1. Always send via bank account, debit card, or Venmo balance. Switching away from credit card saves you 3% on every single transaction.
2. Use standard transfer instead of instant. Unless it is urgent, waiting 1–3 business days saves you 1.75% every time you withdraw. On $500 that is $8.75 saved per withdrawal.
3. Batch your withdrawals into one transfer. Let payments accumulate and transfer once per week. One fee on a larger amount is always cheaper than multiple fees on small amounts.
4. Use a Business Profile if you sell regularly. A Business Profile reduces your goods and services fee substantially compared to receiving payments on a personal account.
5. Only use Goods & Services when purchase protection is actually needed. For trusted friends and family, a standard personal payment is always free. Reserve G&S for strangers where buyer and seller protection matters.
6. Check your default funding source before every payment. The most common and most avoidable Venmo fee mistake is accidentally sending via credit card because it was left as the default. Verify your payment method before hitting send.
Frequently Asked Questions
How much is the Venmo instant transfer fee? 
The Venmo instant transfer fee is 1.75% of the transferred amount, with a minimum charge of $0.25 and a maximum cap of $25.00. A $100 transfer costs $1.75. A $500 transfer costs $8.75. Any transfer of $1,429 or more costs exactly $25.00 the maximum. Standard transfers (1–3 business days) are always free.
What is the Venmo goods and services fee in 2026?
For a Business or Charity Profile, the fee is 1.9% + $0.10 per transaction. For Tap to Pay (NFC contactless in-person), the fee is 2.29% + $0.10. The fee is always paid by the seller and automatically deducted from the received amount. Buyers pay nothing extra.
How much does Venmo charge for a $100 instant transfer?
Venmo charges $1.75 for a $100 instant transfer (1.75% × $100). You receive $98.25 in your bank account. Use the free standard transfer to receive the full $100 it takes 1–3 business days.
How much does Venmo charge for a $500 instant transfer? 
Venmo charges $8.75 for a $500 instant transfer (1.75% × $500). You receive $491.25. The free standard transfer gives you the full $500 in 1–3 business days.
Does Venmo charge a fee for credit card payments? 
Yes. Venmo charges a flat 3% fee when you send money using a linked credit card. You can completely avoid this by switching your funding source to a bank account, debit card, or your Venmo balance all of which are always free.
Does Venmo charge a fee for business transactions? 
Yes. Venmo charges 1.9% + $0.10 per payment received through a Business or Charity Profile for goods or services. Tap to Pay (NFC contactless) transactions cost 2.29% + $0.10. There is no monthly fee or setup fee.
Is Venmo free for buyers on goods and services payments? 
Yes. Buyers pay no G&S fee. The seller absorbs the full transaction fee. However, if the buyer funds their payment using a credit card, Venmo's standard 3% credit card fee applies to the buyer's transaction.
How do I calculate Venmo fees manually? 
Credit Card: Amount × 0.03. Instant Transfer: Amount × 0.0175 (minimum $0.25, maximum $25). Business G&S: (Amount × 0.019) + $0.10. Tap to Pay: (Amount × 0.0229) + $0.10. Or use the Venmo fee calculator at the top of this page for instant results.
Can I avoid Venmo fees completely? Yes  for personal payments. Send using your bank account, debit card, or Venmo balance (never a credit card), and use the standard transfer when withdrawing to your bank. Both are always free. For business goods and services payments, a fee always applies when you receive payments, but using a Business Profile keeps the rate as low as possible.
Is Venmo cheaper than PayPal for business? Yes, for US domestic transactions. Venmo charges 1.9% + $0.10 per business G&S transaction, while PayPal charges 3.49% + $0.49 for the same type. On a $500 sale that is a saving of over $5 per transaction with Venmo.
What ATM can I use with Venmo for free? Any ATM within the MoneyPass network is free with your Venmo Mastercard Debit Card. Out-of-network ATMs cost $2.50 per withdrawal. ATM balance inquiries are free at any ATM, in or out of network.
Does Venmo charge for check deposits? Only for faster deposits. Payroll and government checks cost 1% (minimum $5) for the fast option. All other accepted check types cost 5% (minimum $5) for the fast option. If you choose the standard 10-day hold, all check deposits are free.
